1. Introduction

Welcome to Study Tracker ("we", "our", "us", or "the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our web application at https://study-five-umber.vercel.app.

Study Tracker is operated by Darshan Derkar (contact: darshanderkar20@gmail.com). By registering or using Study Tracker, you agree to the collection and use of information described in this policy.

If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please do not use the application.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information — collected when you register:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (used for admin contact during access approval only)
  • Password (hashed and managed securely by Firebase Authentication — we never see your plaintext password)

Study & Usage Data — generated as you use the app:

  • Topics and chapters you log for study
  • Study session dates and times
  • Exam dates you configure (e.g. JEE Mains, JEE Advanced)
  • Daily study hours you record
  • Revision completion status and streaks

Google Account Data (Optional) — only if you choose to connect Google Calendar:

  • Your Google account email address (displayed in the app to confirm which account is connected)
  • An OAuth access token that lets the app create calendar events — stored only in your browser's localStorage and never sent to our servers

3. Google API & OAuth Data Use Key Section

✓ Study Tracker's use of information received from Google APIs strictly adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

OAuth Scopes We Request:

ScopeWhy We Need It
calendar.events To create spaced-repetition revision reminder events directly in your Google Calendar when you request it. We do not read, modify, or delete your existing events.
openid / email (userinfo) To display your connected Google account email in the Calendar settings panel. No profile data is stored on our servers.

What we DO with your Google data:

  • Create revision reminder calendar events according to the Ebbinghaus spaced-repetition schedule (Day 1, 3, 7, 14, 30, 60, 90)
  • Display your Google email in the app to confirm which account is connected

What we DO NOT do with your Google data:

  • ✗ We do not read, access, or copy your existing Google Calendar events
  • ✗ We do not store your Google OAuth token on our servers or in any database
  • ✗ We do not share your Google account information with any third parties
  • ✗ We do not use your Google data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond the calendar feature
  • ✗ We do not transfer your Google data to other apps or services
  • ✗ We do not allow humans to read your Google data except as required by law

⚠ Your Google OAuth token is stored only in your browser's localStorage. It is never transmitted to our servers. You can revoke access at any time via Google Account Permissions or by tapping "Disconnect" inside the app.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your Study Tracker account
  • To generate personalised spaced-repetition revision schedules
  • To sync your study data across your devices in real time
  • To create Google Calendar revision events (only when you explicitly request it)
  • To allow the administrator to review and approve account access requests
  • To improve the application's features and reliability
  • To respond to your support requests

We do not use your data for advertising, marketing to third parties, or any purpose not listed above.

5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to anyone.

Your data is only accessible to:

  • You (the account holder) — full access to your own data
  • The app administrator (Darshan Derkar) — limited access for access-approval and technical support only

We may disclose your information only if required by law, court order, or to protect the safety of users. Our infrastructure providers (Firebase, Vercel) process your data as sub-processors under their own privacy commitments — see Section 11.

6. Data Storage & Security

All study data and account information is stored in Google Firebase Firestore, a cloud database operated by Google LLC. Firebase employs AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS encryption in transit.

Authentication is handled by Firebase Authentication, which manages password hashing and session security. Google OAuth tokens are stored only in your browser's localStorage — never on our servers.

We encourage you to use a strong, unique password and to use the built-in Backup feature regularly.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. To request deletion of your account and all associated data:

  • Email darshanderkar20@gmail.com with the subject "Delete My Account"
  • We will permanently delete all your data from Firebase within 30 days of your request and confirm via email

Google Calendar events already created in your Google Calendar must be removed manually through Google Calendar, as those exist within Google's systems.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — view all your personal data stored in the app
  • Correct — update inaccurate information via your profile
  • Delete — request permanent deletion of your account and data (see Section 7)
  • Export — download your study data using the built-in Backup (JSON export)
  • Revoke Google Access — disconnect Google Calendar via Google Account Permissions or in-app "Disconnect"
  • Object — object to any processing you believe is unlawful

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at darshanderkar20@gmail.com.

9. Cookies & Local Storage

Study Tracker uses browser localStorage (not advertising cookies) to store:

  • Your UI preferences (dark/light mode theme)
  • Cached study data for offline access
  • Your Google Calendar OAuth access token and expiry (on your device only)
  • Backup reminder dismissal state

We do not use advertising cookies, cross-site tracking, or third-party analytics cookies. Firebase may set functional cookies for session management — see Firebase Privacy Policy.

10. Children's Privacy

Study Tracker is designed for JEE aspirants (typically aged 16–18). We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child under 13 has registered, please contact us at darshanderkar20@gmail.com and we will promptly delete such information.
