1. Introduction

Welcome to Study Tracker. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our web application.

By registering or using Study Tracker, you agree to the collection and use of information described in this policy, including our strict security measures for account sharing prevention.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information (Registration):

  • Full name, Email address, and Phone number.
  • All new registrations are placed in a pending queue and are manually verified by an administrator before access is granted.

Study Data:

  • Topics, chapters, and revision logs generated as you use the spaced-repetition logic.

3. Fraud Prevention & Hardware Tracking Security Strict

To protect the integrity of the platform and enforce fairness, we utilise advanced security tracking to prevent account sharing and unauthorized access:

  • Hardware Fingerprinting: When you log in, we generate a unique cryptographic fingerprint of your physical device (based on your screen hardware, rendering engine, and system properties). This explicitly prevents circumventing the 2-device login limit via Incognito mode or clearing cookies.
  • Network Geolocation (IP Tracking): We track the approximate geographic location (City and Country) of your active internet connection at the exact moment of login. This is displayed directly on the Administrator’s Live Dashboard.
  • Suspicious Activity Flagging: If our system detects simultaneous active sessions across widely differing geographic locations (e.g., logging in from two different cities at the same time), your account is automatically flagged for Suspicious Credential Sharing in the Admin Panel, which may result in an immediate session kick or account revocation.
  • Ghost Session Pruning: To ensure slots are not falsely consumed, our servers run automated cron jobs to purge abandoned sessions older than 12 hours.

✓ Why we do this: These mechanisms are enforced entirely for abuse-prevention and enforcing the stated 2-device limit per student.

4. Google API & OAuth Data Use

If you optionally connect Google Calendar, we strictly adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy.

  • Scope requested: calendar.events. Used exclusively to create spaced-repetition reminder events in your calendar.
  • We do not read your existing events. We do not store your OAuth access token on our servers (it remains entirely in your local browser storage).

5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. Your data is accessible only by you and the system administrator. We utilise Google Firebase processing infrastructure to secure your data.

6. Data Retention & Account Deletion

If your account is permanently revoked or deleted by an administrator, your active sessions are instantly terminated globally. If you wish to request total deletion of your analytics and study data, please email our support team directly. Revoked accounts may re-request access via the login portal.
