1. Acceptance of Terms

By registering for or using Study Tracker, you agree to be bound by these Terms of Service. If you do not accept these terms, specifically regarding our strict anti-sharing policies, you may not use the service.

2. Account Approval & Device Limits Strict

Study Tracker operates on a closed, administrator-verified basis.

  • Upon registering, your account is placed into a "Pending" queue. You cannot access the service until the administrator manually approves your specific account ID.
  • Device Limiting: Each student account is biologically restricted to a strictly enforced maximum of 2 simultaneous devices (e.g., one Laptop, one Phone).
  • Opening "Incognito Mode" tabs, spoofing user agents, or frequently flushing your browser cache will actively consume your device slots and permanently lock you out of your account until a manual Admin Reset is executed.

3. Account Sharing & Violations

We actively monitor our systems to prevent fraudulent usage and safeguard our infrastructure limits.

⚠ Zero-Tolerance Sharing Policy: Attempting to share your login credentials with classmates or friends is strictly prohibited. If our geolocation systems detect your account logging in from multiple opposing geographic zones simultaneously, or exceeding hardware boundaries, your account will instantly be flagged in the Admin Dashboard for "Suspicious Credential Sharing".

Violation of the sharing policy will result in an immediate hardware kick, revoking all of your current internet sessions instantly.

4. Termination & Revocation

The administrator reserves the final right to revoke, suspend, or permanently delete any student account at their sole discretion. Total deletion of an account removes all historical study analytics and permanently invalidates all session tokens associated with that email address. If an account is deleted, the user may re-request access by logging in, triggering a new administrative Request.
