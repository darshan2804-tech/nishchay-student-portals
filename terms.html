2. Description of Service

Study Tracker is a free web application designed to help JEE aspirants implement the Ebbinghaus spaced-repetition learning method. The service includes:

  • Study topic logging and tracking
  • Automated spaced-repetition revision schedules (Day 1, 3, 7, 14, 30, 60, 90)
  • Optional Google Calendar integration for revision reminders
  • Performance tracking, streaks, and study analytics
  • Weekly email progress reports (opt-in)
  • Study history and PDF exports
